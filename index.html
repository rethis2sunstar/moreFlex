My Quest for Hot Sauces

This page is devoted to my love of chicken wings and finding great sauces to be used at home

Jack Astors Habanero Sauce

This sauce has this nice hint of mango that makes the chicken wings sing. It adheres well but there is so little of it in the bottle.

Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Wing Sauce

The classic sauce but it's missing the sour notes normal with hot sauce

Valentines Hot Chili Sauce

A great chili sauce. Good for chilli, chicken wings, fajitas and anything else that you want to up the heat. Use as a condiment or as an ingredient
